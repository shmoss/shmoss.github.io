About Me

I'm a GIS analyst for Jones, Lang, LaSalle, a commercial real estate firm, and a recent graduate of UW-Madison's GIS and Cartography Master's program. My research examined the relationship between voting behavior and crime at the neighborhood-level in Chicago.

Broadly, I am interested in the intersection of cartography, GIS, and urban geography. If you have a cartography or GIS project you'd like to talk about, get in touch with me here!
